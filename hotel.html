Where to stay

We reserved a block at the Hilton Financial District Hotel. The rate is $219/night.

This hotel is 6 blocks away from the venue (albeit 4 of those are uphill). It is two blocks away from the #1 bus stop at Sacramento and Kearny.

If you're feeling adventurous, the California St cable car stop is 3 blocks away at California and Kearny. In both cases, make sure to get off at Powell St.
